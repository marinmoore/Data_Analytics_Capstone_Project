# Data_Analytics_Capstone_Project
## Tracking Antisemitic &amp; Anti-LGBTQ+ Hate Crimes in the U.S.

Over the past 10 years, there has been a stark increase in hate crime incidents in the United States, disproportionate to national population growth. The rise in antisemitic and anti-LGBTQ+ hate crime activity has been particularly notable. The main objective is to investigate in-depth factors which may be correlated with this rise in hate crimes as a whole, and more specifically with antisemitic and anti-LBGTQ+ incidents.
